About EasyWay Ordering
Our Mission Statement: Empowering small and medium size restaurants to offer their customers the same online ordering convenience as their larger counterparts.

EasyWay Ordering is leading an online-ordering revolution. We are reconnecting restaurants with their customers, and providing them with the same tools as their big-brand competitors.

Our clients want to strengthen their brand identity, streamline their ordering processes and increase customer loyalty.
