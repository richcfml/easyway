About Group Ordering
Group ordering allows you to order food with
friends, co-workers or any group of people!
Just click the "Start Group Order" button to get started.

Fill in the details, and invite people to join the order via email or Facebook. You can have one person pay for the order, or many - it's up to you!

Once your order has been started, we notify all the members. You get to see(in real time!) who has ordered what, and who still needs to order.

You can even message the group members directly from the shopping cart.

When all orders have been placed, simply click "Submit Group Order", and you're done!
About Favorite Orders
Favorite orders make ordering your usual even easier.

Want to add the order you are working on to your favorites?
Just click the favorites tab, click "Add New", fill in the details and you're done! Next time you are craving that same meal - just navigate to the Favorites tab and click "Re-Order."

Need to edit an existing favorite order? Just locate it in the list, and click on "Edit."

Another great feature to try is our Quick Favorite function. Just text in the name of any saved favorite order to the appropriate number, and your order will be on it's way! Look for the Quick Favorite logo to identify participating restaurants.
