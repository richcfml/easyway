FAQ
Answers to our clients most frequently asked questions.
Q. How Do I Access My Admin Panel?
Locate your admin panel by visiting our website and clicking Client Login, in the upper righthand corner.
Q. How Do I See My Orders?
Login into your admin panel, click on the Orders tab. Click on “New Orders”. Make sure to approve your new orders, to keep the system from getting back-logged.
Q. How Do I Edit My Menu?
To edit your menus, login in to your Admin panel. Click the Menus, then click the menu name you wish to edit.
Q. How Do I Provide a Refund?
Locate your admin panel by visiting our website and clicking Client Login, in the upper righthand corner.
